The leaderboard is a recurring **weekly options trading competition** with real prizes. Open it with the **trophy icon** in the top navigation.

## How the competition works

Rankings are based on **cumulative notional volume** of options traded during the round — profit or loss doesn't matter, so every trade counts toward your standing.

- Rounds run **Friday 12:00 AM to Thursday 11:59 PM (EST)**, with a live countdown to the weekly reset.
- The top three traders get a podium at the top of the page; your own row is highlighted wherever you rank.
- Standings refresh every few seconds while the page is open.
- A **Rules & Prizes** modal on the page has the full details for the current round.

## Prizes

| Place | Prize |
|-------|-------|
| **1st** | $100 |
| **2nd** | $50 |
| **3rd** | $25 |
| **4th–10th** | $5 each |

Prizes are paid in **pUSD** directly to your wallet.

## Rankings

Each row in the leaderboard shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **#** | Your rank for the current round |
| **Trader** | Display name (set on your Profile page) |
| **Contracts Traded** | Number of options contracts traded this round |
| **Volume** | Notional volume traded this round |

You appear on the leaderboard after your first options trade of the round.
