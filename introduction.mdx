Theta Labs is the home of **options on prediction markets**. Buy cash-settled call options on a market's probability — **Yes Calls** and **No Calls** — to get leveraged exposure with your risk strictly capped at the premium you pay. Alongside options, you can trade **spot** YES/NO shares on Polymarket markets from the same account and balance.

Everything runs on a single **pUSD balance**: deposit USDC on Polygon and it converts automatically — no external wallet, no manual transfers between platforms.

<Warning>
Options on Theta Labs are **real-money trading**. The premium you pay is the most you can lose on a contract you buy, but losses are real. Trade responsibly.
</Warning>

## What you can do

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with one click">
    Sign in with your email, Google account, or an external wallet. Theta Labs uses embedded wallets — a wallet is created for you automatically, with no extension to install and no seed phrase to manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit USDC">
    Send USDC on **Polygon** to your deposit address. It converts to **pUSD** — your single tradeable balance — automatically. No transfer steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade options">
    The **Options** page is your landing view. Pick a market, choose Yes Calls or No Calls, select a strike and expiry, and buy with defined risk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade spot">
    Switch to the **Spot** page to buy YES or NO shares on Polymarket markets across politics, sports, crypto, economics, and more.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key concepts

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Yes Call / No Call** | A cash-settled call option on a market's Yes (or No) price. Pays off when the price finishes above your strike at expiry. |
| **Strike** | The probability threshold your option is struck at, shown as a percentage. |
| **Premium** | The price you pay for a contract — and the most you can lose as a buyer. |
| **pUSD** | Your tradeable balance. USDC deposits on Polygon convert to pUSD automatically. |
| **YES/NO share** | A binary outcome token on a spot market, worth $1 if the event resolves in that direction, $0 otherwise. |
| **Available** | The pUSD cash in your account that's ready to deploy in new trades. |
